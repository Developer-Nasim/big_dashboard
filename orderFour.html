Credit Card Gateway

Please select the appropriate gateway below

To accept credit cards online using Authorize.net, you must have an Authorize.net Gateway account. Authorize.net is an E-commerce gateway that sends online credit card transactions from your website to your current payment processor. We recommend that you contact your payment processer rep to setup an Authorize.net account. *Make sure Test Mode is turned OFF in your Authorize.net account.
